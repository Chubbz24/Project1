CCTV Cameras

CCTV Cameras (Closed-Circuit Television), are used as security devices. CCTV cameras record everything that it sees and sends that information to the user/owner, they are also sending this information to bigger and wider networks, and are also connecting us into the net. They connect us to the net by helping facial recognition and so on. CCTV cameras are often found in public places, i.e supermarkets, malls, stores, etc.
